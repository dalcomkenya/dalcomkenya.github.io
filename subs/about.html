Who We Are

DALCOM KENYA LIMITED is a Kenyan Based Limited Company registered under the Companies Act (Cap. 486) in the Republic of Kenya in May 2013. Before then, it operated as Dalcom Enterprises Limited.

What We Do

Our area of specialization is in motor vehicle industry where we come in as manufactures of certified and qualified governors .Our product is called OMATA V2030GAR which is a certified speed limiter and recorder by Kenya bureau of standards.

Our Mission

Our mission which is tied in our vision is to ensure safety standards are maintained on our roads with a cutting edge on motor vehicles so that the government can achieve its vision 2030.
